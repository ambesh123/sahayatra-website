About Sahayatra

Sahayatra is an innovative carpooling platform designed to connect commuters in a safe, affordable, and eco-friendly way. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, cut down carbon emissions, and make daily travel more social and efficient.

Founded by passionate individuals who care about sustainable transportation, Sahayatra aims to become the go-to choice for shared mobility in India. Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional traveler, Sahayatra offers a seamless platform to share your ride or find one that fits your schedule.

We're committed to continuously enhancing our services to ensure the best user experience, security, and reliability. Your journey matters — and with Sahayatra, you’re never traveling alone.
